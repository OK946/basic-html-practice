Drumming is VERY fun!

Once you know what you are doing, drumming can be very fun.

Equipment

New equipment can be very expensive, so try and get it used from people you know or from second-hane stores.

Types of Drums and Cymbals

It can be confusing when you don't know what type of drum or cymbal you want to be hitting.

  • High-Hat: this pair of symbals are on top of each other and have a pedal to open and close the cymbals.
  • Snare Drum: The snare is good for adding something at the end of a beat to make it less basic.
  • Base Drum: This is used using a pedal and is used for a similar purpose as the snare.

How to start drumming

I recommend following these steps to start drumming:

  1. Look online for tutorials and videos of people drumming.
  2. Practice basic techniques like how to hold the sticks.
  3. Start practicing alone and be prepared to fail.
